Pune Institute of Computer Technology, (PICT) is an engineering college in Pune, India. Established
by the Society for Computer Research and Training in 1983, it was the first institute to introduce a
Computer Engineering branch amongprivate colleges in the Maharashtra state. It is ranked
amongst the topmost private engineering colleges in the country.
